Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Overview

MakerStats is a statistics tracking app for MakerWorld. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Data We Collect

Local Data Only — All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Usernames you track: Stored locally on your device
  • Statistics data: Downloaded from MakerWorld and stored locally
  • Points data: If you log in, your points are fetched and stored locally
  • App preferences: Settings like refresh intervals stored locally

What We DON'T Collect

  • No personal information
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No data sent to our servers (we don't have any!)
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using platform-specific secure storage (SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS/macOS). We do not operate any servers or cloud storage.

Third-Party Services

MakerStats connects to:

  • MakerWorld (makerworld.com): To fetch public profile statistics and points data

We do not share any data with third parties.

MakerWorld Login

If you choose to log in for points tracking:

  • Your login session is handled directly by MakerWorld
  • Cookies are stored locally on your device
  • We cannot see your MakerWorld password

Children's Privacy

MakerStats is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data Deletion

To delete all data:

  • Open Settings in MakerStats
  • Tap "Clear All Data"

Or simply uninstall the app.

Changes

We may update this policy. Check this page for the latest version.

Contact

For questions, open an issue on our GitHub repository.
